ABOUT

For the longest of time, I've wanted to do something with software and music, even when I have zero knowledge about music and hold the guitar like I would hold a spoon. But what's fun in an activity that you already know, right? (blatant ignorance)

Music is fun, and it should be fun, at least for me. Which is why the name of the entire project is something right from a meme-book (?) and the names of the two sub-apps are puns in themselves. Good luck figuring out what I came up with first - the names or the projects.

Because of my indecisiveness, there are two products right now, which thankfully didn't go up to three due to lack of time. The first is the main app - a realtime music maker with audio recording and downloading and collabrative properties. The second is a musical game, which um, does not involve any music. It's a guessing - something I remember playing with my brother a lot - only, instead of song titles, we did movie titles.

I look forward to improving the apps to something less comic and more valuable in the future.
